一、ChatGPT4中的DALL E3 的电影海报

DALL·E 3可以直接在画面中识别和生成文字。非常适合用来创作电影海报。用下面的电影海报提示词的通用模板：

[Movie Title] [Genre] movie poster, focusing on [Central Theme or Characters]. [Setting Elements] set the scene, with [Additional Atmosphere Details] enhancing the ambiance. The [Adjective for Title] title prominently displayed.

[电影名称] [电影类型]电影海报，聚焦于[核心主题或角色]。[场景元素]营造氛围，[其他氛围细节]进一步增强情感。[形容标题的词]的标题醒目地展示。

你也可以创作出最史诗般的电影海报！

注意，[电影名称] 必须用英文，DALL·E 3目前似乎还无法准确识别中文。

二、ChatGPT4下的DALL E3的实例

比如：
Nuovo Cinema Paradiso 剧情电影海报，聚焦于一个年轻男子与小镇电影院和老板的深厚关系，以及他后来追寻自己电影梦想的历程。老旧电影院、胶片和小镇风光作为主要场景元素，带有一丝怀旧和温暖的氛围。激动人心的标题显著展示。

Nuovo Cinema Paradiso drama movie poster, focusing on a young man’s enduring relationship with a small-town cinema and its proprietor, and his subsequent journey in pursuing his filmmaking dreams. Vintage cinema setting, film reels, and small-town ambiance set the scene, imbued with an air of nostalgia and warmth. The emotive title is prominently displayed.

再比如：

肖申克的救赎 剧情电影海报，深入探索一名银行家在被错判谋杀后，如何在肖申克监狱中寻找希望和自由。铁窗、图书馆和石墙为主要场景元素，氛围压抑但又充满希望。引人注目的标题醒目展示。

The Shawshank Redemption drama movie poster, delving into the journey of a banker who, after being wrongly convicted of murder, seeks hope and freedom within the confining walls of Shawshank Prison. Iron bars, a library, and stone walls set the scene, creating an atmosphere that is both oppressive and hopeful. The captivating title is prominently displayed.
